Pace Caddie

Support

Pace Caddie is a simple golf pace-of-play timer built for quick glances outdoors.

Use this page for basic help, App Store support information, and privacy links.

How to use Pace Caddie

  1. Set your target time for 18 holes. The default is 4:15.
  2. Tap Start Round when you tee off.
  3. Watch the large target-hole number to see where you should be to stay on pace.
  4. Use Prev and Next to manually indicate your actual hole.
  5. Use Pause if the round stops for a break, weather, or a delay you do not want counted.

Understanding the pace status

On pace Shows time left on the current target hole.
Ahead of pace Shows when your manually selected hole is ahead of the target hole.
Behind pace Shows when your manually selected hole is behind the target hole.

Common questions

Does Pace Caddie use GPS?

No. Pace Caddie does not use GPS or location tracking.

Does the app collect data?

No. Pace Caddie does not collect personal data, use accounts, show ads, or include tracking.

Can I change the pace target?

Yes. Use the settings control to choose a preset or adjust the target time.

Why does the app show “left on hole”?

When your actual hole matches the target hole, Pace Caddie shows how much time remains before the target pace moves to the next hole.
