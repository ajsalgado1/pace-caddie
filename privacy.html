Pace Caddie

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 24, 2026

Pace Caddie is a simple golf pace-of-play timer. The app is designed to work without accounts, location tracking, advertising, analytics, or third-party tracking tools.

Information we collect

Pace Caddie does not collect personal information. The app does not ask you to create an account, enter your name, share your location, or provide contact information.

Round and pace data

Pace Caddie uses timing and hole information only to show your pace during a round. This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Tracking and advertising

Pace Caddie does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, cookies, cross-app tracking, or third-party tracking.

Data sharing

Because Pace Caddie does not collect personal information, we do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

Children’s privacy

Pace Caddie is a general-audience sports utility and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

If the app’s data practices change in the future, this policy will be updated to describe those changes.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, use the developer contact information listed with Pace Caddie on the App Store.
